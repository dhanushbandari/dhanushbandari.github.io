About

Hey there, it’s Dhanush Bandari..!

Nice to meet you. I don’t know how to describe myself, but still here I’m typing about myself, giving the worst introduction about myself with beautiful words. So, I’m from a small city who has big dreams and vision. By the way, I’m still in the process of figuring out how to make it real. I think I talk too much..! Let’s begin with the proper introduction of mine, so buckle up and bear with me..

I’m an Electronics and Communication Engineering student who has a strong interest in VLSI, Embedded, and IoT automation. And of course, I do know coding sometimes it’s for designing in Verilog, and typing C/C++ for Arduino and ESP32. Sometimes scripting and automating in Python, sometimes using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s getting fun every day. I always think that I’m a mixture of software and hardware, and I am..! And I love to know how the hardware and software interact with each other. I’m always in the mood of doing something and learning new things. If my brain gets stuck, I just go out and love to explore nature. I mean trekking where I get my inner peace back. Even a small walkout will work, but that’s for the time being. Nature will heal everyone if we enjoy without harming; that’s what I believe. By the way, I love to read fiction novels and self-help books. Those are my source of imagination.

I’m documenting this to express how I learn and apply things, and to tell myself that you did well when the times and things are hard. I always remember a sentence of a poem that I learned in 6th std:

“TRY AND TRY AGAIN BOY YOU’LL SUCCEED AT LAST”

Well, I hope you got to know so much about me. I prefer to describe myself by doing things instead of talking or typing words. I think it’s not meant to talk in this way because I prefer action over talk. Well, if you’re a geek who loves learning new things and exploring yourself in something new, you’re welcome to sneak in.
