## Why i Choose to write it?

I’m writing this because I’ve personally felt how powerful nature can be. It heals you from the inside and connects with your emotions. I’ve been through it, and that’s why I just wanted to share something real with my words and the moments I’ve captured

## Peace In Chaos..!

After joining Engineering you'll make two new friends : 
>Stress and Pressure 
{: .prompt-info } 

And trust me they will never leave you.To deal with them, you’ve got to figure things out on your own, because these aren’t things college really teaches you.

So yeah,today i just wanted to share something from my personal experience. Whenever i feel overwhelmed or if something keeps me bothering me for long time, I choose nature to heal myself, and honestly, it works every single time. I love being in nature, surrounded by greenery everywhere not like a full on Amazon rainforest, just simple, peaceful green spaces. I really enjoy trekking, walking along mountain trails, and reaching the top… just standing there and breathing in fresh air. That feeling is more satisfying than anything else

## when does nature heals you ?

For me, nature heals when I’m out there, walking for hours, covering kilometers. All the stress and overthinking just fades away by the time I reach the top or destination. It feels like I've conquered something like I've won against the world and everything that was bothering me.
